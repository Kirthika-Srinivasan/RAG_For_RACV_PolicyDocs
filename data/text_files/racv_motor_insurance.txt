RACV Complete Care Motor Insurance Policy Overview

RACV Complete Care Motor Insurance is issued by Insurance Manufacturers of Australia Pty Ltd and distributed by RACV Insurance Services Pty Ltd. The policy provides comprehensive cover for damage caused to your vehicle and liability cover for damage you may cause to other people's property or vehicles.

What Is Covered:
- Accidental damage to your vehicle including collision, theft, and attempted theft
- Damage caused by fire, storm, flood, or other listed weather events
- Vandalism and malicious damage to your vehicle
- Liability cover for unintentional damage caused to third party vehicles or property

What Is Not Covered:
- Damage resulting from wear and tear or mechanical breakdown
- Damage caused while the vehicle is being driven by an unlicensed driver
- Loss or damage occurring while the vehicle is used for hire or reward unless declared
- Consequential loss including loss of income or depreciation in vehicle value

Membership and Renewability:
A member is defined as a person or business holding a renewable product with RACV. Renewable products include Emergency Roadside Assistance, RACV Club Membership, Insurance, Emergency Home Assist, and personal loans. Membership card colours reflect continuous years of membership: Blue for zero to four years, Bronze for five to nine years, Silver for ten to twenty-four years, Gold for twenty-five to fifty years, and Gold 50 for fifty-one years or more.
