RACV Emergency Roadside Assistance Coverage

RACV Emergency Roadside Assistance provides members with 24/7 breakdown support across Victoria and Australia. Members can request help via the RACV App or by calling the assistance line at any time of the day or night.

Key Coverage Inclusions:
- Unlimited service calls for flat batteries and flat tyres
- Towing up to 20km within metro Melbourne to the nearest service centre
- Emergency fuel or diesel delivery up to 5 litres
- Locksmith services if keys are locked inside the vehicle
- Electric vehicles assisted at no extra cost

Important Exclusions:
- Breakdown services or towing as a result of a vehicle accident or collision are not covered
- Assistance for caravans and trailers is not included in basic plans
- If your vehicle is unroadworthy, RACV may refuse assistance
- A 24-hour waiting period applies before new members can access plan benefits

Plan Tiers:
RACV offers three levels of roadside assistance cover: Basic Care, Extra Care, and Total Care. Extra Care and Total Care plans include caravan and trailer assistance plus a minor parts allowance. Members with five or more continuous years of membership receive a loyalty discount of up to 20 percent applied automatically at renewal.
